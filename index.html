The Lord of the Rings

This is a trilogy of epic fantasy adventure films. They are directed by Peter Jackson. The movies are based on the novel of J.R.R.Tolkien

It sets in the Middle-earth and the main caracter is the hobbit Frodo Baggins

The Fellowship of the Ring

In Middle-earth, the wizard Gandalf the Grey discovers that the ring that the Hobbit Frodo Baggins possesses, which was bequeathed to him by his uncle Bilbo and which allows him to become invisible, is in reality the unique Ring sought by Lord Sauron because it would allow him to regain all his power.

The Two Towers

After the Fellowship of the Ring explodes, Frodo and Sam continue their march.

They are attacked by Gollum who tries to steal the ring. Defeated, he cooperates and offers the two Hobbits to take them to Mordor. He knows the road well.

The Return of the King

The armies of Sauron attack Minas Tirith.Gandalf strives to bolster the broken forces of Gondor, Theoden urges the warriors of Rohan to join the fight. But despite their courage and loyalty, the forces of Men are no match for the legions of enemies that descend upon the kingdom... Frodo must rely on Sam and Gollum, while the Ring continues to tempt him...
